# Cognitive Architecture

Phase 1 cognition is deliberately minimal and deterministic.

## Survival Layer

The Survival Layer protects the account first. It limits trade permission based on balance, spread, risk, daily loss, and account mode.

## Chimp Layer

The Chimp Layer is the simple action layer. It can score basic market conditions and pass only clear, bounded signals to risk and execution.

## Deferred Layers

Oracle, Ghost Trading, external services, and AI behavior are intentionally deferred.
